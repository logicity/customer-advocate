- made recommendations to existing and prospective customers on products and services to suit particular needs.
- solved service-related issues.
- used CRM to administer and update customers' accounts.

Problem-solving

I am in my element when helping people solve problems. I push to ensure an issue is resolved and I derive a great deal of satisfaction in putting a smile on a customer’s face.

I love consulting with customers, evaluating their needs and gauging their opinion on how a product or service can serve them better.

Empathy

I am an empathetic person. I have a knack for seeing the point of view of the other party. Putting myself in the customers' shoes comes naturally to me.

4) A good Customer support company and why they are good.

Bluehost customer Support (Bluehost is a web hosting company).

- Bluehost Promises Kept:

 I once registered a domain with bluehost, but I didn't like the name anymore soon after registration. Changing the name wasn't possible, so I had to terminate the account. Bluehost has a no question-asked, no run-around, money-back guarantee policy. Therefore, I requested for a refund. My money was refunded within twenty-four hours. I was happy they kept their word, and I re-registered the domain with them.

- Helpful FAQ section and prompt response:

 Services are ideally designed to run smoothly for customers, but issues could come up sometimes. Bluehost's FAQ section was quiet helpful in understanding their services. But, then I needed some clarifications, so I had to contact Bluehost's live chat. The live chat operator responded in good time and was courteous too.
